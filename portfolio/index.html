Introduction

I've navigated the complexities of project coordination, mastered the art of technical troubleshooting, and played a key role in bringing critical system implementations to life. ALX Virtual Assistant programme

The ALX Virtual Assistant (VA) Program is an 8-week, fully digital learning experience designed to equip individuals with the essential skills needed to launch a career as a virtual assistant. It focuses on both soft and practical skills, including workplace etiquette, communication, project management, and leveraging AI and other key workplace apps. The program also emphasizes learning by doing, connecting with others, and receiving mentorship from experienced VAs.
